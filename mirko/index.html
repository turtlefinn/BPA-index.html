OUR SERVICES

Explore unlimited possibilities

CARMAZING consists of highly qualified people always eager to meet your every automotive needs. Our experts ensure your vehicle stays fit to hit the road, assuring you of a safe, comfortable and secure journey. We provide maintenance services such as:

OIL CHANGES

A full-service oil change involves the replacement of your vehicle's motor oil with the proper grade, which may be conventional, synthetic, synthetic blend, or high-mileage oil. Additionally, your old oil and oil filter will be changed and recycled.


ENGINE INSPECTIONS

Engine examination entails determining the precise symptom that causes the engine to knock. Engine inspection is primarily intended to examine the general condition of the engine as well as its parts. The main purpose of engine inspection is to evaluate the important components for wear and tear.

TUNE UPS

Once the issue has been identified, the tune up process replaces all the old and rusty parts with new parts in your vehicle.




MAINTENANCE INSPECTIONS

During a maintenance inspection, we make sure to check things like the air filter, tire pressure, headlights, and we replace them with new ones if needed.

BATTERY INSPECTIONS

A battery inspection consists of a visual and physical examination of your battery. Your technician will visually inspect your battery for corrosion, cracking, and casing issues, among other things.

Transmission Service

A full transmission service, includes inspecting the sump pump or pan (if possible), replacing or cleaning the filter, cleaning the pan, and fitting it with a new pan gasket.
